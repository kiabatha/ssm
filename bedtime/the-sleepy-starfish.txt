The Sleepy Starfish!

On the coast of Greece, Nikos found a starfish that glowed at night. It whispered dreams of the sea, carrying him into waters filled with silver light. Nikos swam with dolphins, played with glowing shells, and drifted through waves that sang lullabies. The starfish promised to guard his sleep, shining softly beside him. When morning came, Nikos woke with joy, knowing the sea itself was his friend.

---The End
