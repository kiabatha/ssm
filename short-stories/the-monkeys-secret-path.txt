One rainy afternoon, he found a narrow path behind a waterfall. It led to a dry cave filled with glowing stones and soft moss.

When a storm hit the jungle, Tiko remembered the cave. He guided his friends—parrots, frogs, and other monkeys—through the hidden path.

They stayed safe and warm until the rain stopped. From that day on, the cave became a secret shelter. Tiko had turned curiosity into courage.
